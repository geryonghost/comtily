Cybersecurity for SMB

Problem:
Small Business can be affected by the same cyber criminals that big business can. However, the outcomes can be dramatically different. Most small businesses that are affected by cyber crime go out of business because it is too expensive to fix or their reputation is lost.

Solution:
Utilize tooling that is commercially available to create a solution that offers malware protection/alerting, security patch management, system backups, and security trainings.

  • Use a tool like JumpCloud to enable software deployments in addition to patch management.
  • Work with an anti-malware vendor such as Norton, McAfee, or others for malware proection/alerting.
  • A company like KnowBe4 is good at both testing and training for security related issues like phishing.
  • Backups are complicated, but there are solutions out there that can be utilized.
  • Automate the installation, deployment, and configuration of these tools to make the process seemless for the consumer
  • Keep the price as low as possible while making enough to pay contributors and employees a fair wage
